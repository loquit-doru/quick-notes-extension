⚡ Quick Notes - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 21, 2026

TL;DR: Your notes stay on YOUR device. We never see them. No tracking. No analytics. No cloud.

Overview

Quick Notes is built with privacy as a core principle. We believe your notes are yours alone.

Data We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ Personal information
  • ❌ Note contents
  • ❌ Browsing history
  • ❌ Usage analytics
  • ❌ Any identifiable data

Data Storage

  • ✅ All notes are stored locally on your device using IndexedDB
  • ✅ Notes never leave your browser unless you explicitly export them
  • ✅ No cloud sync = no data transmission

Permissions Used

  • storage - To save your notes locally on your device
  • activeTab - To capture page context (URL/title) when you create a note
  • tabs - To get current tab information for context capture

Third-Party Services

  • ExtensionPay - Used only for processing card payments. We do not store or see payment details.
  • Base Network - Used only for verifying crypto payments. Only transaction hashes are checked publicly on the blockchain.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
